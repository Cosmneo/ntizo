About This Service

Experience professional hair styling tailored to your unique look and preferences. Maria Costa brings over 8 years of expertise in hair styling, from classic cuts to modern trends. Whether you are preparing for a special event or simply want a fresh everyday look, this service delivers salon-quality results at your convenience.

The session begins with a personal consultation to understand your hair type, lifestyle, and desired outcome. Using premium products and professional-grade tools, every detail is carefully attended to ensure you leave feeling confident and looking your best.

What's Included

  • Personal consultation to discuss your style goals
  • Wash & deep conditioning treatment
  • Professional cut and styling
  • Premium finishing products applied
  • Personalized style advice and maintenance tips
