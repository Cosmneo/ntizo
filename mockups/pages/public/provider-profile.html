About

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, I specialize in hair styling, coloring, and bridal makeup. I graduated from the Maputo School of Beauty Arts and have worked with top salons across the city before becoming an independent provider on Ntizo.

My passion is helping people look and feel their best for every occasion. Whether it's an everyday cut, a bold new color, or the perfect bridal look, I bring creativity, precision, and care to every appointment. I work at your location for maximum convenience.
