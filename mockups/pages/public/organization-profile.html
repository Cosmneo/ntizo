About

FixaSol Reparos is a trusted home services company based in Maputo, specializing in plumbing, electrical installations, and general home maintenance. Founded in 2020, we have grown into a team of 6 certified professionals serving the greater Maputo and Matola area.

We pride ourselves on reliability, transparent pricing, and quality workmanship. All our technicians are licensed and insured, and we offer a satisfaction guarantee on every job. From a leaky faucet to a full electrical rewiring, no job is too small or too large for our team.
